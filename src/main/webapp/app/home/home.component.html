PROTECT is a H2020 Marie Sk≈Çodowska-Curie Action (MSCA) Innovation Training Network (ITN) with the aim of developing new ways of empowering users of digital services to understand the risks they take when they go online and offering new tools that enable companies to incorporate data protection into their digital services.
In this context, we did an analysis of the existing privacy policy languages and data protection vocabularies that can be used to represent the information items required by GDPR rights and obligations. These pieces of information can be something as simple as defining a controller's identity or more complex concepts such as a data protection impact assessment.
More information about the surveyed languages and ontologies can be found on the LANGUAGES and ONTOLOGIES pages, respectively. For each analysed solution, there is a brief description of the language or ontology and also links to additional documentation and available RDF serializations. There is more information about the authors of the solutions, when it was first created and last updated, about the projects and / or research groups where it was developed and, when available, examples of implementations that are using it.
